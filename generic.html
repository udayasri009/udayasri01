3-Month Internship Project – ExcelR Institute

HR ATTRITION ANALYSIS

The objective of this HR Analytics project is to evaluate workforce performance,employee retention and organizational efficiency by analyzing employee demographic data, attrition trends, and job-related factors.Using SQL-based data analytics,HR datasets are extracted, cleaned, transformed, and queried to generate accurate KPIs related to employee count, attrition rate, tenure distribution, compensation patterns, department-wise performance, and workforce diversity. The analysis focuses on identifying high-attrition departments, critical job roles, experience-level trends, and factors influencing employee.

In addition,the project aims to design and implement a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard using business intelligence and data visualization tools to convert raw employee data—such as Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Average Age, and Average Tenure—into actionable insights. Interactive dashboards visualize employee trends over time, compare departmental attrition, analyze salary and performance distributions, and highlight workforce composition by age, gender, education, and job level. These insights support data-driven decision-making in talent management, workforce planning, retention strategy development, and overall organizational performance improvement.
